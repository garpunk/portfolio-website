Content

Home page

Here at garrettvoiceart, I aim to provide professional sounding voice-overs for my clients. People will come to this site for my professional voice over acting, narration, ad-reading, and audiobook production. This site will answer questions such as, what kind of services do I need, how much of these services do I need, and what competitive prices can I receive these services at? People will come to this site because of the quality of the voice over services provided, as well as the competitive price and speed in which the work can be done.

Images for the Home page

What kind of services are offered?

I offer a variety of voice over services. I can provide animated narration for character voice overs, including characters featured in animated works. I also provide clean, professional voice overs for ad-reads and narrations, delivering clear, concise diction for products and promotionals. I am also proficient in audiobook narration, combining animated character performances with rich and engaging book narration.
