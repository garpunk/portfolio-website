My Services

What kind of services are offered?

I offer a variety of voice over services. I utilize the cutting-edge technology provided in the Studio One Digital Audio Workstation (DAW). I can provide animated narration for character voice overs, including characters featured in animated works. I also provide clean, professional voice overs for ad-reads and narrations, delivering clear, concise diction for products and promotionals. I am also proficient in audiobook narration, combining animated character performances with rich and engaging book narration.
